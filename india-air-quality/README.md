# india-air-quality

Analysis of PM2.5 air quality data across 277 Indian cities, exploring pollution trends, cigarette equivalents, seasonal patterns, and tier-wise comparisons.

## Data Sources

- CPCB (Central Pollution Control Board) air quality bulletins
- Historical PM2.5 summary data (1998-2024)
- Delhi-specific historical PM2.5 readings

## Methodology

1. Explore and clean historical PM2.5 datasets (`01_data_exploration.ipynb`)
2. Generate visualizations: metro timeseries, annual averages, cigarette equivalents, seasonal patterns, COVID impact (`02_data_visualization.py`)
3. Tier-wise city analysis: AQI distributions, category breakdowns, top 20 most polluted cities (`03_tier_analysis.py`)
